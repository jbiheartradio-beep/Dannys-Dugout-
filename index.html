Meet the Coach

Hi, I'm Danny Lieber.

I’m an experienced slowpitch softball player in the Tampa Bay area with a deep background in coaching and player development. My goal is simple: to help adult players of all skill levels improve their swing, sharpen their fielding, and dominate their local leagues.

Six years ago, I founded the South Tampa BP Club—a community built on the love of the game and the grind of getting better. That mission is what led me to create Danny’s Dugout, providing high-level, personalized instruction for the modern slowpitch athlete.

"Whether you are looking to hit your first home run or you're an advanced player looking for elite swing metrics, I’ve got the tools and the experience to get you there."

Common Questions

Where do the lessons take place? ▼

We utilize various local parks and fields across Tampa and St. Pete. Once you book, I will coordinate with you to find the most convenient field.

Can I bring a teammate? ▼

**Absolutely.** For advanced players, I highly recommend bringing 1 or 2 teammates for a **Group BP session**. This allows for more swings with recovery time and splits the cost.

What if it rains? ▼

Florida weather happens! If we get rained out, we will simply reschedule your session to the next available weekend at no extra cost.
