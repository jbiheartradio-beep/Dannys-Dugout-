Meet the Coach

Hi, I'm Danny.

I’m an experienced slowpitch softball player in the Tampa Bay area with a deep background in both playing and coaching. My passion for development started at Palma Ceia Little League, where I coached baseball and learned the value of helping players build true confidence on the field.

Six years ago, I founded South Tampa BP Club. What started as a practice group has grown into a thriving community where I’ve helped countless men and women refine their swing. That mission is what led me to create Danny’s Dugout.

My approach is simple: I combine proven techniques with real-game experience to help you reach your full potential. Whether you're a beginner trying to find your swing or a veteran looking for that extra power, I’m committed to helping you improve while ensuring you have a great experience.
