Meet the Coach

Danny Lieber

English & Español

I am an experienced slowpitch softball player in the Tampa Bay area with a deep-rooted passion for the game that spans decades. My coaching journey began on the local fields of Little League, where I spent several years developing young athletes and mastering the essential fundamentals of the sport.

Throughout my time coaching Little League, I learned that every successful player is built on a foundation of proper mechanics and mental discipline. I have taken those years of experience and adapted them specifically for the adult slowpitch game.

Six years ago, I founded the South Tampa BP Club to provide a community for local players to get the consistent reps they need. Today, through Danny’s Dugout, I provide high-level, personalized instruction for adult athletes who want to start dominating their leagues.
