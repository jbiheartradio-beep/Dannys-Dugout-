Meet the Coach

Danny Lieber

English & Español

I’m an experienced slowpitch softball player in the Tampa Bay area with a deep-rooted passion for the game that goes back decades. My coaching journey didn't start with the pros—it started on the local fields of Little League, where I spent years developing young athletes and mastering the fundamentals of the game.

During my time coaching Little League, I learned that every great player is built on a foundation of proper mechanics and mental discipline. I’ve taken those years of experience—teaching everything from high-pressure situational play to the perfect swing plane—and adapted them specifically for the adult slowpitch game.

Six years ago, I founded the South Tampa BP Club to create a space for local players to get the reps they need. Today, through Danny’s Dugout, I provide high-level, personalized instruction for adult athletes who want to stop "just playing" and start dominating.

Common Questions

We use various local parks across Tampa, Clearwater, and St. Pete. I coordinate with you to find a field that's mutually convenient.

**Yes.** I offer Group BP at the same hourly rates. It’s a great way to get high-volume swings and split the cost with teammates.
